The Dog who can flip

Chapter 1

There was a dog named Spike. Spike could do many tricks which included jumping, wagging and running. Other dogs can do these tricks but there's one only Spike can do. Spike was a dog who can flip.


Chapter 2

All the children gathered at Spike to see him flip. Spike got nervous seeing the children. "Strangers" he exclaimed, "Why are they gathered around me." Spike took a fall rather than his usual flip and ran away. "Spike!!", his owner shouted, "Where are you?" Spike's owner was a young girl who just entered elementary school. She had gotten Spike as a gift from her father when she was younger.


Chapter 3

She searched in the woods for Spike because her and Spike play near the woods. She found Spike hiding behind a bush. "Spike?", she shouted, "I miss you, please come back." Spike came out of the bush and licked his owner. "Spike, where were you?" she asked. Spike barked at her with anxiety. "I'm sorry Spike, I didn't know my friends made you scared, let's go back."


Chapter 4

"We're very sorry Spike, we didn't know we made you nervous." they all said. "We just wanted to see you flip." "It's ok.", his owner smiled, "You could have told me, Spike gets nervous when he's surrounded by strangers." The children laughed and played with Spike for a while. Spike did a flip in excitment as the children awed. "Spike is the greatest dog ever!" they all screamed.
